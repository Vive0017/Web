Health in Australia

Introduction

This visualization aims to explore health in Australia through publicly availiable data published by the Australian Bureau of Statistics[ABS]. The visualisation tries to answer the following questions. Are there any differences between the health of males and females? In which state does one live the longest? How are the habits of Australians affecting their life expectancy?
